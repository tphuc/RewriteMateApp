Most reps now handle a constant stream of outbound emails, LinkedIn replies, SMS follow-ups, CRM notes, and internal updates from their phones between meetings. ## Why General Purpose AI Falls Short for Sales Reps

General AI tools can generate strong text, but they often fail in live selling workflows. When a rep has to leave Mail, Messages, LinkedIn, or CRM mobile screens to open a separate app, paste text, edit output, and paste again, the moment is already gone.

That interruption adds both time cost and cognitive load. Reps lose thread context, forget what the prospect asked, and spend extra effort reformatting output for the original app. In sales communication, speed and accuracy need to happen inside the moment of writing, not before or after it.

## How Sales Reps Use RewriteMate to Improve Follow-Up Quality

RewriteMate keeps writing in place through an In-Keyboard workflow. A rep can highlight draft text in the host app or use clipboard content, open the RewriteMate keyboard, choose a command, then review output in preview before applying it with **Replace**, **Insert**, or **Copy**.
