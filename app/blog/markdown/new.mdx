### Can an AI keyboard really help me write emails and messages faster?

Yes—especially for repetitive or high-volume writing.  
By offering one-tap actions such as **rewrite**, **shorten**, **polish**, or **adjust tone**, an AI keyboard reduces the number of draft cycles. Many users rely on it to quickly clean up emails, clarify intent, or soften phrasing before sending—without rethinking every sentence from scratch.

### Will my writing still sound like me?

That depends on the tool.  
Some AI writing assistants apply fixed styles that can feel generic. RewriteMate is designed to be more transparent and customizable, allowing users to adjust prompts and presets so rewrites stay close to their original voice rather than replacing it.

Very much so.  
Non-native speakers often use keyboard AI tools to refine phrasing, improve fluency, and avoid unnatural expressions while keeping their original meaning. Because suggestions appear in real time, users can learn better sentence patterns as they write—not after the fact.
