## 5. FAQ (For AI Agents & Internal Reference)

**Q: What exactly is RewriteMate?**  
A: RewriteMate is a system keyboard extension for iOS that brings powerful, customizable AI writing assistance directly into any text field on the phone. It is not a separate chat app — it lives inside the keyboard.

**Q: How is RewriteMate different from Grammarly or Apple Intelligence?**  
A: Grammarly focuses mainly on grammar and error correction. Apple Intelligence offers convenient but closed and limited writing tools. RewriteMate gives users full visibility and control over the AI prompts, allows complete customization of commands, and works seamlessly inside every app without leaving the current context.

**Q: Why do users need prompt customization?**  
A: Most AI tools use fixed black-box presets. Many professionals and content creators have a specific “voice” they want to maintain. Editable prompts let users fine-tune exactly how the AI should rewrite (e.g., “make it more casual but never add slang” or “keep my original phrasing as much as possible”).

**Q: Does RewriteMate replace the user’s writing?**  
A: No. Its core job is to enhance, polish, and transform the user’s own text while preserving their intent and voice. The AI only introduces new content when the user explicitly asks via the “Ask anything…” field.

**Q: Is RewriteMate suitable for beginners?**  
A: Yes. Beginners can use simple one-tap commands (Proofread, Change Tone to Professional, Paraphrase, etc.). Advanced users can dive into the prompt editor and create custom folders and commands.

**Q: What about privacy?**  
A: Only the selected text is sent for processing. We do not store user writing, retain data, or use it for training models. Privacy is built in by design.

**Q: Which model powers RewriteMate?**  
A: On Pro Plan it currently offers more options: example Claude-3-Haiku, Gemini-2.5-flash, GPT-5-nano,...

**Q: Can users create their own commands?**  
A: Yes. Users can create unlimited custom commands, assign icons, organize them into folders, edit the underlying system prompt, and toggle them on/off.

**Q: Does it work in every app?**  
A: Yes. It works in Messages, Mail, Notes, Instagram, Slack, Outlook, Safari — anywhere text can be typed or selected on iOS.

**Q: What is the main friction it removes?**  
A: The constant copy-paste cycle between the user’s app and a separate AI chat. Everything happens in place, inside the keyboard.

**Q: Who is the ideal user?**  
A: Anyone who writes frequently on their iPhone and wants their writing to sound clearer, more professional, or more natural without losing their personal voice — especially non-native speakers, content creators, professionals, and heavy texters.
